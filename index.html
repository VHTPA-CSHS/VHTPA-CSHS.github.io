The start of a long-lasting commitment to computer science.

CSHS is short for Computer Science Honor Society and follows a student-run organization. It's a unique place where students can get in touch with computer science and join together to learn, grow, and lead in their own communities for the opportunities computer science can offer. Members of CSHS will discover many different ways of acquainting themselves with computer sciences and learn the skills and connections that will be necessary in their academic and life goals.

Requirements

  • Must be 10th, 11th, or 12th
  • B or higher in an exisitng CS class
  • GPA of 3.5 or above
